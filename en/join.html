Join the movement

Whether you want to give your time, become an active member or support our projects long term, there is a place for you with us.

How to contribute

Three ways to join us

Volunteer

Give your time during collection drives, awareness campaigns or support for the families ARDTTEMP accompanies.

Active member

Take part in the life of the association, its decisions and field activities as a statutory member of ARDTTEMP.

Benefactor / Partner

Support our programmes financially or in kind as a company, institution or committed individual.

How it works

Three simple steps

01

Fill in the form

Share your details, region and how you would like to contribute.

02

Chat with our team

A member of the executive board will contact you to better understand your availability and expectations.

03

Get started

You join the field teams or associative life according to your chosen membership format.
