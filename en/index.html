Let's Transform Our Environment Together

ARDTTEMP brings together communities, entrepreneurs and public authorities around a single mission: reducing the impact of plastic waste by turning it into resources, while supporting the most vulnerable people in our communities.

Our mission

Research in the service of ecological and inclusive transformation

Association for Research and Development of Ecological Plastic Waste Transformation Techniques, ARDTTEMP partners with communities to reduce the impact of plastic waste on the environment. We promote and popularize transformation practices among entrepreneurs, and support communities affected by plastic pollution.

Our commitment goes beyond the environment: we provide unconditional support to elderly people, under-schooled women, and orphaned or needy children, guided by a logic of solidarity and sustainable development aligned with Cameroon's national objectives.

What we do

Four pillars of action, one ambition

Collection & awareness

Systematic collection operations of used plastic bags and waste from households and markets.

Technical transformation

Research and dissemination of transformation techniques: PET flakes, eco-friendly paving stones and tiles.

Inclusion & education

Our "One Youth, One Diploma" project supports young single mothers toward the CEPE, BEPC, Probatoire and Baccalauréat.

Support for the vulnerable

Unconditional support for elderly people, under-schooled women, and orphaned or needy children.

Our process

From collection to useful resource

Every plastic bag collected follows a precise journey before becoming a resource for communities and for the association's operations.

Collection

Organized pickup of plastic waste in neighborhoods and markets.

Sorting & washing

Separation by material type, cleaning and preparation of the waste.

Flaking

Transformation of PET into flakes reusable by local industry.

Manufacturing

Production of eco-friendly paving stones and tiles from treated waste.

Local impact

Revenue generated funds our social programs and the association's operations.

Flagship project

One Youth, One Diploma

Since its founding, ARDTTEMP has come a long way. The "One Youth, One Diploma" project has helped young single mothers earn their diplomas — CEPE, BEPC, Probatoire and Baccalauréat — across three regions of Cameroon: Centre, East and Adamawa.

This program reflects our core belief: protecting the environment and empowering vulnerable people go hand in hand.
